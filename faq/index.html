How can I join and start playing?

We play all of our games on the Zeqa Network. This requires you to have the Bedrock edition of minecraft installed on your device. Keep in mind that double-clicking is prohibited on Zeqa so you might have to tweak your mouse settings or install DC Prevent to prevent double clicks.

How does tiering work?

There are 4 distinct tiers in CZB, Tier 1 through 4, with Tier 1 being the most skilled and Tier 4 being the least skilled. Once you think you are ready to be evaluated, open a ticket and a Tier-checker will evaluate your skill.

What are the match rules?

There are only three maps which are considered ranked by the community, Lunary, Ender and Manor. You are required to hippo on all 3 maps. Mining out hippos and border winging are also prohibited. Other than that, all Zeqa rules apply as well.

How do I become staff?

We are currently not accepting staff applications. However if you do feel like you can contribute and bring something unique to the table, please contact our head admin @Oaics on Discord.

How do I get on the Tier List?

Every single tiered person will be added on the Tier List.

Whats the practice world?

The practice world is a tool for practicing hippoing, winging bypassing and much more. The download is over on our Discord. Check out this showcase video for more information.
