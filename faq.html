  • What is Competetive Zeqa Bridge?

    Competitve Zeqa Bridge is the largest Bedrock Bridge community. We play our games on the Zeqa Network and feature an advanced training program, monthly tournaments, a practice world, a unique tier system and much much more. For more information please join our discord server!

  • How do I join?

    You can join the community using our permanent invite link (dsc.gg/zeqabridge) or our vanity, discord.gg/zeqabridge, but the vanity link depends on how many boosts we have.

  • Rankings

  • How does ranking work?

    There are multiple steps to getting ranked on the tier list/getting a tier role. First of all, there are 4 ranks.

    1. Tier 1
    2. tier 2
    3. Tier 3
    4. Tier 4
    With Tier 1 being the best and Tier 4 being the lowest skilled. Once a tier checker evalutaees your skill, you will be placed accordingly. Players who are unranked are called members.

  • What is the tier list?

    The CZB Tier List is a visual way of showingcasing our best players. Everyone gets placed on the tier list once they get tier checked. If you are skilled enough to be in the top 10 players, your name will be displayed in the Discord for everyone to see!
