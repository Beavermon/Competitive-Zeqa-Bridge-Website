  • General

  • What is Competetive Zeqa Bridge?

    Competitve Zeqa Bridge is the largest Bedrock Bridge community. We play our games on the Zeqa Network and feature an advanced training program, monthly tournaments, a practice world, a unique tier system and much much more. For more informatio please join our discord server!

  • How do I join?

    You can join the community using our permanent invite link (dsc.gg/zeqabridge) or our vanity, discord.gg/zeqabridge, but the vanity link depends on how many boosts we have.

  • How was this website made?

    This website was made by Beavermon, and is made using a combination of Javascript, Css and Html. I am by no means a web desginer so expect some minor bugs. Inspiration for the sight was taken from Radio and CandyCup. All assets are free to use and the code is open-source on my Github account.

  • Rankings

  • How does ranking work?

    There are multiple steps to getting ranked on the tier list/getting a tier role. First of all, there are 4 ranks.

    1. Tier 1
    2. tier 2
    3. Tier 3
    4. Tier 4
    With Tier 1 being the best and Tier 4 being the lowest skilled. Once a tier checker evalutaees your skill, you will be placed accordingly. Players who are unranked are called members.

  • What is the tier list?

    The CZB Tier List is a visual way of showingcasing our best players. Everyone gets placed on the tier list once they get tier checked. If you are skilled enough to be in the top 10 players, your name wuill be displayed in the Discord for everyone to see!

  • How do I get tier checked?

    In order to get tier checked, you have to be in the Discord server. Once you are there, navigate yourself to the 「📝」𝐓𝐢𝐞𝐫-𝐂𝐡𝐞𝐜𝐤𝐬 channel and open a ticket. Once you are there, a trained tier checker will arrange a session to evaluate your skills. Don't sweat it, just play your best and have fun. Once you are done, you will be ranked shortly. Happy playing!
